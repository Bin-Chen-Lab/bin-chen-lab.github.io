Bin Chen

Principal Investigator

Prior to this position, Bin was a postdoctoral scholar in Dr. Atul Butte’s lab at Stanford University. Bin trained as a chemist in college, worked as a software engineer before graduate school, trained as a chem/bioinformatician in graduate school, worked as a computational scientist in Novartis, Pfizer and Merck. He received his PhD in informatics at Indiana University, Bloomington.
