My long-term goal is to develop methods and tools to discover new therapeutics for cancers by leveraging big data. This goal aligns with the recent Precision Medicine Initiative and the Cancer Moonshot. I have been building computational pipelines for the translational cancer research comprised of four major components (patients, tissues, in vitro models and in vivo models) (right figure). All these components should be coherently connected in order to find effective treatments more efficiently.
